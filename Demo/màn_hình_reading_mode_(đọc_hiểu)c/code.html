The Future of Personalized Learning

This is the main content area where the full script of the video is displayed. It's formatted like a newspaper or an ebook to make it easy to read. Users can interact with the text. When they tap on a word, a dictionary lookup will appear. When they tap on a sentence, the sentence will be highlighted, and a mini audio player will appear to play the corresponding audio. The text is white on a dark background for optimal readability in Dark Mode.

The core idea is to transform passive video consumption into an active learning experience. Each word is a potential learning opportunity. For instance, clicking on the word "consumption" would trigger the dictionary.

Highlighting an entire sentence like this one will bring up the mini audio player at the bottom of the screen. This allows learners to focus on pronunciation and intonation for specific phrases, reinforcing auditory comprehension alongside reading skills.

The interface is designed to be minimal and unobtrusive, ensuring the focus remains squarely on the content. The AI-powered features, such as the summary tool, are available on-demand, providing powerful assistance without cluttering the primary reading view. This blend of focused design and intelligent features aims to create a deeply effective and enjoyable language learning environment.
